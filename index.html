Auto-connecting earbuds:

Press and hold both touch sensord 3+ seconds to power ON. The Left and Right earbud blinks blue/white indicating ready to pair to your device.

looks_two

Connecting bluetooth:

Select "JLab JBuds Air Icon" in your device settings. Voice prompt "Bluetooth connected" followed by solid white lights wlil indicate you are connected.

Button functions
  • TURN ON / OFF:
    Press & hold 5+ seconds.
  • VOLUME DOWN: click.
  • PLAY / PAUSE: 2 click.
  • TURN ON / OFF:
    Press & hold 5+ seconds.
  • VOLUME UP: 1 click.
  • ANSWER: 1 click.
  • HANGUP: 2 clicks.
  • REJECT INCOMING CALLS:
    Press & held I + second.
  • SIRI (iOS) or "0K GOOGLE" (ANDRIOD):
    2 clicks.
  • TRACK FORWARD:
    Press & hdd 1+ seconds.
Voice prompts

  • "Hello": power ON

  • "Earbuds joined": Earbuds paired to each other

  • "Ready to Pair": JBuds Air are ready to pair to Bluetooth device

  • "Bluetooth Connected": Earbud is connected to device

  • "Battery Full": 100-75%

  • "Battery Medium": 75-25%

  • "Low Battery": Less than 25% When power decreases to 10%, "Low Battery" Wlil play

  • "Goodbye": Power OFF

Equalizer modes

Click either the right or left earbud 3 times for different EQ settings (JBuds Air will cycle 3 EO settings)

Charging earbuds

Placing the earbuds in the case wil auto power Off the earbuds and start charging.

Solid blue lights will indicate charging and wil tum off after earbuds are fully charged.

Charging case

Connect the JBuds Air to computer or USB 5V output device.

LEDs pulse white when charging and tum solid white when fully charged

Fitting

Try the optional Cush Fins and all ear tips for best fir and proper seal.
